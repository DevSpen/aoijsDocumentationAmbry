  To let the bot listen to the event, add one bot.onTypingStart() callback
  inside your mainfile.
---

# bot.onTypingStart

This callback will trigger when the bot sees someone typing.

Example:

## Usage:

```javascript
bot.typingStartCommand({
channel: "ID", // (Optional)
code: `Your code`
});
```
